Goals

CossiePlay is a family-run business, operating in Hulkstown since 2012. They create outstanding custom-made costumes. This website presents information about the company, its history, monthly party information, prices together with promotion, links to sign-up and the party guideline. This website is intended to increase revenue and boost up the name for CossiePlay by organising monthly party to get more people to sign-up for the party online-increasing both new and regular customers.

Success Evaluation

Bring more young people to know more about cosplay and buy customs.

Target Audience

Cosplayers of all ages but mostly 35-50.
